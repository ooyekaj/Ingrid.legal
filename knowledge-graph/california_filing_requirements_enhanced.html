üìÑ Required Documents Checklist

1. Notice of Motion

  • Specify grounds for motion and hearing date (81+ days out)
    See CCP 1005(b) - Notice Requirements | Format per CRC 2.104 - Document Formatting Standards
    SCSC Local Rule 7.1 - Motion Notice Requirements
  • Include case caption and all party information
    See CRC 2.111 - Pleading Format Requirements
  • State specific relief requested

2. Separate Statement of Undisputed Material Facts

  • Two-column format required per CRC 3.1350
    See CRC 3.1350(f) - Separate Statement Format Requirements | CCP 437c(b)(1) - Material Facts Statement
  • Left column: numbered material facts
  • Right column: supporting evidence with page/line citations
  • Each fact must be followed by evidence reference
  • Cannot incorporate by reference
  • Must be filed as separate document

3. Memorandum of Points and Authorities

  • Maximum 20 pages (CRC 3.1113)
    See CRC 3.1113(d) - Page Limitations | CRC 2.104 - Document Formatting Requirements
  • Table of contents if over 10 pages
    See CRC 3.1113(f) - Table of Contents Requirement
  • Table of authorities
  • Statement of facts
  • Legal argument with case citations
  • Conclusion with specific relief requested

üèõÔ∏è Santa Clara County Local Rules

Key Local Rules for Civil Practice:

SCSC Local Rule 2.3 - Mandatory Electronic Filing

All attorneys must file electronically except for documents specifically exempted by court order.

Cross-reference: CRC 2.253 - Statewide Electronic Filing Rules

SCSC Local Rule 3.1 - Case Management Conference Scheduling

Initial case management conference scheduled within 120 days of filing. Subsequent conferences every 120 days unless court orders otherwise.

Cross-reference: CCP 594 - Case Management Requirements

SCSC Local Rule 3.400-3.402 - Complex Civil Case Procedures

Special procedures for complex civil litigation including mandatory service of guidelines and enhanced case management.

Cross-reference: CRC 3.750-3.771 - Complex Civil Litigation

SCSC Local Rule 7.1-7.3 - Law and Motion Practice

Procedures for law and motion calendar, tentative rulings, and hearing requirements.

Cross-reference: CCP 1005-1013 - Notice and Service Requirements

SCSC Local Rule 8.1 - Informal Discovery Conference

Required before filing discovery motions. Must include joint statement of issues and meet and confer certification.

Cross-reference: CCP 2023.010 - Discovery Conference Requirements
