Required Documents Checklist

0 of 5 documents completed

Notice of Motion (CCP § 437c(a)(2)) High Risk
Specify grounds for motion and hearing date (81+ days out) (CCP § 437c(a)(2)). Must include case caption and all party information (CRC 2.111).
Sanctions Risk: $1,500+ for defective notice (CCP § 128.7)
Authority:
CCP § 437c(a)(2) - Notice Requirements
Separate Statement of Undisputed Material Facts (CRC 3.1350) High Risk
Two-column format required (CRC 3.1350(d)). Left column: numbered facts. Right column: evidence citations (CRC 3.1350(f)).
Common Error: Incorporating by reference (not allowed) (CRC 3.1350(g))
Authority:
CRC 3.1350 - Separate Statement Format
Memorandum of Points and Authorities (CCP § 437c(b)(1)) Medium Risk
Maximum 20 pages (CRC 3.1113(d)). Table of contents required if over 10 pages (CRC 3.1113(f)). Include table of authorities (CRC 3.1113(f)).
Format Requirements: 12pt Times New Roman, double-spaced (CRC 2.104)
Authority:
CRC 3.1113 - Motion Brief Requirements CRC 2.104 - Document Format Requirements
Supporting Evidence Medium Risk
Declarations must be on personal knowledge. Exhibits must be authenticated. Discovery responses properly certified.
Tip: Separate binding if over 25 pages
Proof of Service Low Risk
Method of service specified. Date/time of service. Names and addresses of all served parties.
Requirement: Declaration under penalty of perjury
