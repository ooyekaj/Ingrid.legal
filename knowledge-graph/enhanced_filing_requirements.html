Required Actions:

  • a. MANDATORY: Plaintiff must serve Complex Civil Guidelines with summons and complaint
    See CCP 412.20 - Service Requirements | CRC 3.1350 - Complex Case Procedures
    SCSC Local Rule 3.400 - Complex Civil Case Guidelines Service Requirement
    Guidelines Document: Available at https://www.scscourt.org/system/files/civil/complexcivillitigationguidelines.pdf
  • b. Joint case management statements required (Form CM-110 NOT acceptable for complex cases)
    See CRC 3.722 - Case Management Statement Requirements | CCP 594 - Case Management Conferences
    SCSC Local Rule 3.401 - Complex Case Management Statement Requirements
  • c. Electronic filing mandatory for all represented parties in complex cases
    See CRC 2.253 - Electronic Filing Requirements | Gov. Code 68150.5
    SCSC Local Rule 2.3 - Mandatory Electronic Filing
    Must use Tyler Technologies Odyssey File & Serve platform for all filings except specifically exempted documents
  • d. Must clear hearing dates with opposing parties BEFORE contacting Complex Litigation Clerk
    Procedure: Email department7@scscourt.org with proposed dates after conferring with all counsel
  • e. Tentative rulings posted online by 2:00 p.m. day before hearing
    URL: https://www.scscourt.org/tentative-rulings
    Check Department 7 tentative rulings page
  • f. Objections to tentative rulings must be filed by 4:00 p.m. day before hearing or ruling becomes final
    See CCP 1019.5 - Tentative Ruling Procedures
    SCSC Local Rule 7.3 - Tentative Ruling Objection Process
  • g. Ex parte applications discouraged except unusual situations - advance reservation required
    See CCP 1005(b) - Ex Parte Application Requirements | CRC 3.1200-3.1207
    Must call (408) 882-2170 by 10:00 AM for same-day ex parte reservation
  • h. Discovery motions require Informal Discovery Conference (IDC) before filing
    See CCP 2023.010 - Discovery Conference Requirements
    SCSC Local Rule 8.1 - Informal Discovery Conference Procedures
    Schedule IDC through department7@scscourt.org with joint statement of issues
  • i. Must actually meet and confer (in-person/phone/video) - not just letters
    See CCP 2016.040 - Meet and Confer Requirements
  • j. Case management conferences every 120 days at 2:30 p.m. Thursday
    Scheduling: Automatic calendar call - no need to request unless seeking continuance
  • k. Law and motion hearings at 1:30 p.m. Thursday
    Complex matters may require extended time - notify clerk when filing motion
  • l. Continuances discouraged and may be denied even if parties agree
    See CCP 595.2 - Continuance Standards
    SCSC Local Rule 3.402 - Complex Case Continuance Policy
  • m. Must notify court immediately if case settles
    Email department7@scscourt.org within 24 hours of settlement
  • n. Formality, civility and proper decorum required at all times
    Santa Clara County Bar Association Code of Professionalism, Section 2.1
  • o. All electronic devices must be turned OFF in courtroom
  • p. Must stand when objecting or addressing the Court
  • q. Trial counsel must be in courtroom 30 minutes before each morning session
  • r. Witnesses must be ready through 4:30 p.m. or may be deemed to have rested
